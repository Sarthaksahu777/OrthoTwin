ORTHOTWIN FINAL GAP ANALYSIS

Most Important Missing Item
Trusted Patient Alpha image metadata linked to reconstruction: spacing, origin, orientation, and slice thickness.

Most Important Scientific Risk
Precise-looking deterministic proxy outputs may be mistaken for validated biomechanics or clinical predictions.

Most Valuable Existing Asset
The structured state/graph/provenance architecture and honest separation of geometry-derived, rule-derived, estimated, and unknown outputs.

Shortest Path To Research Publication
Publish as a deterministic digital spine-twin prototype and provenance/uncertainty framework. Add radiologist-reviewed measurement validation for a small set of geometry metrics.

Shortest Path To Real Digital Twin
Recover physical calibration, validate measurements, build FEM-ready meshes with materials/boundaries, and fit transition rules to longitudinal data.

Final Status
Digital Twin Prototype, not yet a research-grade biomechanical or clinical digital twin.
