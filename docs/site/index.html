Project Phases

Phase 1 — MRI Browser

Streamlit DICOM viewer for RSNA lumbar spine MRI studies with study/series selection, slice browsing, degeneration labels, and L4–L5 annotation tools.

Try the browser demo →

Phase 2–8 — Digital Twin

Reconstruction, measurements, patient state vectors, spine graph modeling, intervention simulation, and mesh-level transformation outputs.

V1 Showcase

Curated dashboards, architecture diagrams, and audit reports for open-source presentation and research communication.

Scientific Limitations

  • Not a medical device and not for clinical use.
  • No diagnosis, treatment recommendation, or outcome prediction.
  • Physical calibration remains blocked for Patient Alpha.
  • Educational and research prototype only.
